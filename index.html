An addictive casual game where the player starts with a grid beautifully populated with squares in random colors and strives to fill the entire grid with one color before running out of steps. Uses a simple, non-recursive and stateful color fill algorithm using ES6 Map and Set objects and a CSS-only approach using flex boxes and viewport units for maximum responsiveness on different screen sizes.

A faithful digital replication of the yarrow stalk divination procedure that is perhaps 2,500 years old from the Chinese classic, Book of Changes (I Ching). What would have taken 30 minutes can now be completed in two, with data captured at every stage and then based on the hexagram obtained from the procedure, automatically serves the appropriate text from the Book of Changes and Forest of Changes.

An after-sales support platform which simplifies warranty registration by embedding pesky product details in a QR code and saves the need to create a user account with Facebook login. Consumers can then raise support ticket to make warranty claims which customer service officers can then follow up with the customers' contact details.

A Single-Page Application where friends travelling together can collaborate and plan their trip by shortlisting attractions to visit with Google Maps and insert them into a day-by-day itinerary. Each trip is assigned and accessed by a unique identifier, saving the need for logins. After shortlisting attractions with Google Maps, they can then be added to the itinerary by simple choosing the desired day.
